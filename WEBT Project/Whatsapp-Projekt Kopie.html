What Is WhatsApp?

WhatsApp is a texting service between mobile phones as a replacement for the regular SMS text messages.
The major difference between regular SMS text messages and Whatsapp text messages is that Whatsapp is free:
You use the internet connection on your phone.
The application allows voice calls, as well as video calls the sending of text messages images videos and documents and other media.
The service is also accessible from desktop computers.
The service is available for iPhone, Blackberry, Android and Nokia Symbian60-phones.
Over 1 billion people in over 180 countries use WhatsApp1 to stay in touch with friends and family anytime, anywhere.
